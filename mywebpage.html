Joy Gundu

Joy Gundu, is the Nigerian industrialist, who built the International Real Estate company Castle Homes Ng (CastleHomesNg) into the largest manufacturer of electric typewriters and data-processing equipment in the world.

  • In 2010, she obtained a Bachelor's degree in Mass Communications from Olabisi Onabanjo University Ogun State.
  • In 2023s, Joy acted as a coordinator for a Digital Summer Camp for kids.
  • Joy has been teaching French as a second language.
  • She has inducted as a fellow into the Nigerian Institute of Public Relations in 2010.
